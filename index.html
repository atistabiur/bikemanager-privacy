Privacy Policy – Bike Manager

Last updated: February 2026

Bike Manager respects your privacy. This app does not collect personal data.

Information Storage

All bike data, rides, maintenance and expenses are stored locally on your device.

Photos

If you attach photos to records they are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to external servers.

Permissions

  • Camera / Photos – attach photos to records
  • Email – send feedback
  • Internet – open website links

Data Sharing

Bike Manager does not sell or share user data.
